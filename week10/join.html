    Joining the Commonwealth Club opens up a whole new world of learning opportunities and the chance to interact with not only today's headline makers, but also with fellow highly informed and involved citizens.

    member benefits

    In addition to the satisfaction of supporting and being a part of the nation's premier public affairs organization, members of The Commonwealth Club receive the following outstanding benefits:

    • Over 75 free events every year.
    • Discounts off hundreds more events, with up to 60% off nonmember ticket prices.
    • Exclusive access to members-only events.
    • Subscription to our popular magazine, The Commonwealth, which includes transcripts of Club speeches, a calendar of upcoming events and more.
    • Invitations to exclusive Member Socials and special receptions.
